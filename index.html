Features

Built for the speed
of real life

No forms. No friction. Just speak, type, or snap — Orca handles the rest.

🎙️
Voice-first capture
Tap the fin, say anything. "Dentist appointment March 20 at 2pm, remind me the day before." Orca hears the date, the reminder, and files it — all in one shot.
Powered by Sonar NLP
✨
Sonar sorts it
Say "dinner reservation at Carbone Friday" and Sonar files it under Dining, detects the date, and sets a ping. Zero sorting required.
🔔
Pings that fire on time
Not vague reminders — precise pings. Daily, weekly, monthly, or one-time. Orca reminds you exactly when it matters.
📅
Date ranges
"Vacation from June 3 to June 10" — Orca detects both dates and shows your trip across the calendar. No manual entry.
📷
Screenshot & extract
Snap a photo of a menu, whiteboard, or sign. Orca extracts the text and turns it into a searchable memory.
Recipe capture

Paste a URL.
Done.

Find a recipe online? Paste the URL into Orca and it automatically pulls the title, prep time, cook time, and every ingredient — formatted as an interactive checklist you can check off as you cook.

Works with NYT Cooking, AllRecipes, Serious Eats, Food Network, and thousands of other recipe sites.

How it works

Three steps.
That's it.

01
Drop it
Voice, text, photo, or URL. Orca accepts everything in under 5 seconds. No categories to pick, no forms to fill.
"Remind me to call the electrician Monday morning"
02
Sonar sorts it
Our NLP engine reads what you said, picks the right Echo, detects any dates or pings, and files it instantly.
→ Filed to Home 🏠 · Ping set for Monday 9am
03
It finds you
Pings fire at the right moment. Or search anytime — right from your iPhone home screen.
